Cubie

Cubie was developed in a short amount of time as a endless mobile game for Android. The goal of Cubie was to learn mobile development inside of Unity with the inclusion of mobile ad integration with Google Admob and In App Purchases.

The project was publicly released on the Google Play Store in April of 2019 and was supported for 3 months. Since its release, the app has been removed from the store due to TOS changes from Google.
